# Brainstorming the Theme

## Context and Problem Statement
What is the theme of our next application?

## Considered Options
* Space wizards
* Cosmic Horror
* Potion/magical curiosities shop
* Magic mirror
* Fantasy


## Decision Outcome
* Chosen option: “Cosmic Horror”, because it gets the most votes.
